Getting Started

If you are a new user to the dustbin first you have to simply scan the QR, And it will direct you to the website where you can register using your phone number or email Id. Secondly once you have register and later you are using the dustbin and you inserted the plastic bottle in the dustbin and the only you have to scan the QR and it will generate the points as per bottle. There has been a secondary sensor installed in the dustbin which will detect the filling capacity of the dustbin and will automatically send the message to the cleaning authority as well as owner too ,so that waste could take out from the dustbin.
